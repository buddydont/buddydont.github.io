Sprint/Jog Training Programme

High-intensity cardio training combining sprint intervals and jogging sessions to improve endurance, speed, and cardiovascular health.

Training Focus

  • Sprint Intervals: Short, intense bursts for speed development
  • Jogging Sessions: Steady-state cardio for endurance
  • Hill Training: Building power and leg strength
  • Recovery Runs: Active recovery and technique work
  • Pace Work: Training at target race speeds

Benefits

Our sprint and jogging programme enhances cardiovascular fitness, boosts metabolism, and improves overall athletic performance. Suitable for all levels from beginners to competitive athletes.
