Barbells Training Programme

Our barbells training programme is designed for those looking to build serious strength and muscle mass. Using free weights like barbells allows for compound movements that engage multiple muscle groups, leading to efficient and effective workouts.

Key Exercises

  • Squats: Build lower body strength and power
  • Deadlifts: Develop overall posterior chain and grip strength
  • Bench Press: Target chest, shoulders, and triceps
  • Overhead Press: Improve shoulder stability and upper body strength
  • Rows: Strengthen back muscles and improve posture

Benefits

Barbell training promotes functional strength, increases bone density, and helps with weight management. Our experienced trainers will guide you through proper form to maximize results and minimize injury risk.
