Sauna & Steam Bath Programme

Experience the rejuvenating benefits of our premium sauna and steam bath facilities. Heat therapy promotes detoxification, improves circulation, and aids in muscle recovery.

Facilities

  • Traditional Sauna: Dry heat for deep relaxation
  • Steam Bath: Humid heat for respiratory benefits
  • Infrared Sauna: Gentle heat penetration

Benefits
