Jumping Training Programme

Our jumping programme combines plyometric exercises with cardio elements to build explosive power, improve cardiovascular fitness, and enhance overall athletic performance.

Key Exercises

  • Box Jumps: Explosive leg power
  • Jump Rope: Cardiovascular endurance
  • Burpees: Full body conditioning
  • Squat Jumps: Lower body strength and power
  • Lateral Bounds: Agility and coordination

Benefits

Jumping exercises improve bone density, boost metabolism, and develop fast-twitch muscle fibers. Our programme is suitable for athletes and fitness enthusiasts looking to increase power and speed.
