Sparring Training Programme

Our sparring programme provides controlled combat practice in a safe environment. Learn to apply techniques under pressure while building confidence and reflexes.

Training Focus

  • Technical Sparring: Focus on specific techniques
  • Light Contact: Building confidence with controlled intensity
  • Competition Prep: Preparing for fights or tournaments
  • Self-Defense: Practical application of techniques
  • Partner Drills: Cooperative training exercises

Benefits

Sparring develops timing, distance management, and adaptability. Our experienced coaches ensure all sessions are conducted safely with proper protective gear and supervision.
